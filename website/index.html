Production figures from our own solarpanels.

This website presents the production from our own solarpanels. Our solar-array consists of:

  • 2 x Kyocera KD190GH-2YU
  • 2 x Kyocera KD245GH-2YU
  • 6 x Kyocera KD220GH-2YU

for a total of 2190 Wp. The inverter is a Fronius IG30.
The panels are placed on the pitched roof of our house. The inclination of the roof is 35 degrees and its orientation is WZW. Theoretically this array should produce 1752 KwH per year. To accurately measure the total output a DMM ST140 kWH meter has been added.
